Our Story

Founded with a passion for exceptional furniture, Oxford Sofas began its journey with a simple mission: to provide luxury seating solutions that transform every space into a haven of comfort and style.

What started as a small showroom in Lahore has grown into one of Pakistan's most trusted furniture companies. We pride ourselves on partnering with world-class manufacturers and artisans who share our commitment to quality craftsmanship.

Our mission is to make premium furniture accessible to every home, office, and hospitality venue across the country. We believe that a great sofa is not just furniture — it is the heart of a room where memories are made.

Our core values: Quality without compromise, customer-first service, honest pricing, and sustainable practices that respect both people and the planet.
